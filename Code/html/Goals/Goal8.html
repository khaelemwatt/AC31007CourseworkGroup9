Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic growth

How To Help

Invite people with experience in a certain field to create a mentoring group that will help young people better prepare for their future jobs. Be a conscious consumer - if something is cheap is it likely to be hurting people or the planet in some way. Buy from local producers Support local economic growth by choosing to buy from local businesses.
